A Yorkshire Heritage

166 Years of Craiglands

Built in 1859 as one of the country's largest purpose-built hydropathic establishments — still standing above Ilkley Moor.

1857

The Yorkshire Cure

Hydrotherapy at its peak. The waters of the Ilkley hills were praised as "exhilarating and wholesome" — the town a Victorian spa destination.

1859

Built by the Dobson Brothers

Founded by Dr. Henry Dobson and his brothers as one of the country's largest purpose-built hydropathic establishments for the Victorian elite.

Hydro Era

Turkish & Russian Baths

Mustard pads, massage, and Turkish, Russian and electro-chemical baths — all administered under Dr. Dobson's direct supervision.

6 Acres

Lawns & Recreation

Six acres of grounds with croquet, lawn tennis, a bowling alley and a recreational hall — entertainment beyond the treatment rooms.

Late 19th C.

The Tide Turns

Hydropathy slowly fell out of fashion. The Craiglands began to reposition itself, looking ahead to a different kind of guest.

Post-WWI

From Spa to Resort

The hotel transitioned to a holiday destination as Ilkley emerged as one of Yorkshire's favourite weekend escapes.

Wartime

The Turkish Bath Held On

Through the 1940s the hotel kept operating, the original Turkish Bath still in service for guests through wartime.

Modern Era

Reinventions & Refurbishments

Bedrooms updated, function rooms restored, the Conservatory glazed and re-opened — modern hospitality on Victorian foundations.

Today

A Modern Stay

Two AA Rosette dining, 90+ bedrooms, ten function rooms — and the same view of Ilkley Moor through every window.

Events · Banqueting · Celebrations

Events & Banqueting.

Four flexible function rooms — from intimate boardroom dinners in the Dales Room to 280-guest celebrations in the Moorside.

The Moorside

Breath Taking Function Suite

This breathtaking room will blow you away, with its high ornate ceilings, unique feature windows and bountiful character. The original features provide the perfect backdrop to unleash your creative flare. Fully licensed for all ceremonies up to 280 guests, accommodating a wedding breakfast party up to 228.

The Ballroom

Show Stopping Function Suite

If you're looking for a show-stopping function space, you've just found it. With a capacity of 500, built-in stage, grand dance floor and striking chandelier features, this room will tick all your boxes. A room which truly impresses.

The Conservatory

Light-Filled Function Suite

As the name suggests, this room is abundant with natural light from the expanse of windows. The smallest of the rooms but ready to go with a fully equipped bar, it's a blank canvas for you to transform.

The Dales Room

Intimate Function Suite

The smallest of our function suites, located on the first floor with spectacular views of the grounds and surrounding countryside. Ideal for small to medium private lunches or dinners. With full-length windows and air conditioning/heating.

Dine

Brasserie1

Informal, contemporary dining with French windows opening onto the gardens and a clear view across to the Yorkshire moors. A fireplace warms the room through winter; in summer the doors fold back onto the terrace.

Modern interpretations of classic dishes, built on locally sourced produce. Our signature is the award-winning pork sausage from David Lishman of Ilkley — served with colcannon and a grain mustard sauce.

Adjoining the restaurant is the recently renovated Antler Bar & Lounge — a place to settle in with something good in the glass before or after dinner.
