Business at The Craiglands

Conference · Meetings · Success

Our modern and versatile facilities can accommodate from 2 up to 500 delegates. Any of our 10 function rooms can be laid out in a variety of styles to meet individual requirements for all types of events.

Ilkley is easy to reach via the A65, with nearby motorway connections on the M62 and A1(M), plus rail links and Leeds Bradford Airport.
