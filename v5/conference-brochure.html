Beyond the meeting

Day delegate & residential

Choose between a Day Delegate package (8.30am–5pm with everything above) or a 24-Hour Delegate package that includes overnight accommodation, dinner in Brasserie 1, and a full English breakfast the next morning.

Our function rooms also host training seminars, product launches, themed events, Christmas parties and private celebrations. The team will quote any of these on request.
