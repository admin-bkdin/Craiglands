01 — Our Story

A Yorkshire landmark since 1859.

Set against the storied backdrop of Ilkley Moor, Craiglands has welcomed travellers for more than a century and a half. Stone walls, sweeping lawns and cathedral-quiet drawing rooms — every corner whispers of an older, slower England.

Today, sixty-three thoughtfully appointed bedrooms, an acclaimed brasserie, and ballrooms that have hosted generations of weddings continue a quiet legacy of hospitality.

"There is a particular kind of stillness here — the kind only old stones and good company can hold." — Country Living Magazine

01 — Our Kitchen

Yorkshire larder, unhurried.

Our kitchen is built around the people we know — the farmers, smokers, and growers within an hour's drive. Lamb from the moors. Trout from the dales. Vegetables pulled the morning they arrive.

The result is a menu that changes with the season and the weather, and a room that never seems in a hurry.

"The kind of dining room you want to fold an afternoon into."
